Privacy Policy

StreamClipAutoReposter – Personal Clip Automation Tool

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

StreamClipAutoReposter ("Service") is a personal automation tool. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Your Credentials

To operate, the Service requires:

  • Twitch API credentials (Client ID and Client Secret) — to authenticate with Twitch and fetch clips
  • TikTok API credentials (Access Token, Client ID, Client Secret) — to authenticate with TikTok and upload videos

These credentials are stored locally on your device or server and are never shared with third parties.

2.2 Clip Data

When retrieving clips from Twitch, the Service accesses:

  • Public clip URLs, titles, descriptions, and metadata
  • Creator/streamer attribution information

This data is used only to download, process, and repost clips. It is not stored permanently or sold.

2.3 Processed Videos

Reformatted videos (with captions) are stored:

  • Locally on your device or server during processing
  • On TikTok's servers after upload (subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy)

You are responsible for managing retention and deletion of these files.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Analytics or tracking cookies: The Service does not track user behavior.
  • User data from third parties: We do not collect data about viewers or followers.
  • Payment information: This is a non-commercial tool.
  • Browsing history or location data: The Service has no internet connectivity beyond API calls.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for:

  1. Authenticating with Twitch and TikTok APIs
  2. Retrieving, processing, and uploading your clips
  3. Storing temporary files for processing (automatically cleaned up)

We do not:

  • Sell or share credentials with third parties
  • Use data for marketing or profiling
  • Share data with analytics or advertising platforms
  • Store data after processing is complete (unless on TikTok's servers per their policy)

5. Data Retention

  • Credentials: Retained indefinitely (under your control; delete anytime)
  • Processed videos: Retained locally until you delete them; TikTok retains per their policy
  • Clip metadata: Not retained after processing; temporary files are cleaned up automatically

6. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with:

  • Twitch.tv — Public API, subject to Twitch's Privacy Policy
  • TikTok.com — Public API, subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Whisper (if using cloud STT) — subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Other cloud STT providers (optional; your choice)

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices. Review their policies independently.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • Credentials are stored locally, not transmitted to external servers
  • Communication with Twitch and TikTok APIs uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No passwords or sensitive data are logged

You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your API credentials secure (do not share publicly)
  • Revoking API access if the Service is compromised
  • Using the Service only on trusted devices

8. Data Breaches

If you suspect a security issue (e.g., leaked credentials), immediately:

  1. Revoke your API credentials in Twitch and TikTok developer portals
  2. Generate new credentials
  3. Report the issue to the GitHub repository

The Service maintainers are not liable for third-party breaches or misuse of credentials.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years old (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any changes.
